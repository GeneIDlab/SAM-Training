The Following Monday

🚀 Start in the Field

You've done the work. Show up, lead with value, and always leave with a next step scheduled.
This is it — your first day in the field. Everything from this week comes together now. A few reminders as you head out:
→The majority of your sales interactions should be in person. Showing up is what builds trust.
→You're not selling a test — you're helping providers identify at-risk patients before symptoms appear. Lead with stats, not products.
→Always leave with a follow-up scheduled. Never leave without a next step.
→Log every touch in HubSpot — moving the deal stage is what fires all the automations.
→The close: "To get started, all I need is the new account paperwork signed — the BAA and enrollment form."
